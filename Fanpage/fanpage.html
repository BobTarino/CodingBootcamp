BIO

Johnny Cash a.k.a "The Man In Black" was born in Kingsland, Arkansas on February 26th, 1932.

With a signature all-black image, Cash gained the repuation of an outlaw who's music contained themes of sorrow, moral tribulation, and redemption. He became a controversial figure for performing concerts in prisons in the late 50's. and for his public use of drugs and alocohol.

Fusing country, rockabilly, blues, gospel, folk, and rock and roll, Johnny Cash has sold more than 90 million records. He passed away on September 12th, 2003 at the age of 71.
